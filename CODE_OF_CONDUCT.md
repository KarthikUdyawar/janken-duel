# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful and inclusive
- Accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, trolling, or discrimination
- Personal attacks
- Spam or irrelevant promotion

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this code.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it by opening an issue or contacting the maintainer.
